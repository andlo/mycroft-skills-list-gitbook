### _wake-on-lan-skill.Peyos_  
## Description:  
This skill sends a Magic Packet to one of the configured devices to boot it up from hibernation. Wake on Lan has to be active in the receiving device.

### Examples:  
> Boot my computer.  
> Wake up my computer.
