### _bluetooth-speaker-skill.l0drex_  
## Description:  
Activate bluetooth and pair new devices to play some music with mycroft.
You need to install mycroft on a device that supports bluetooth.
Uses alsa.

### Examples:  
> Pairing mode.  
> Pair device.  
> Activate bluetooth.
