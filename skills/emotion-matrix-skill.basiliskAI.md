### _emotion-matrix-skill.basiliskAI_  
## Description:  
Give mycroft emotions! using a simple vad matrix, mycroft can remember what emotion he has and change them at your command.

### Examples:  
> Feel happy.  
> Feel angry.  
> How are you feeling.  
> How are you doing.  
> How you feeling.  
> How you doing.  
> Increase arousal.  
> Decrease valence.
