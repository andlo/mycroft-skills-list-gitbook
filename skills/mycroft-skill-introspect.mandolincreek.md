# Mycroft Introspection Skill  
### _mycroft-skill-introspect.mandolincreek_  
## About:  
This skill will allow interactive discovery of Mycroft's capabilities:

- skills
- intents
- vocabulary.

> What can you do?  
> What are your skills?
