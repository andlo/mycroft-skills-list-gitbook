### _mycroft-volume.syner1_  
## Description:  
Control the volume of Mycroft with verbal commands or by spinning the physical
button on a Mark 1.  
  
  
### Platform:  
 ![Mark I](../.gitbook/assets/mark-1-icon.png)   
### Examples:  
> Turn up the volume.  
> Decrease the audio.  
> Mute audio.  
> Set volume to 5.  
> Set volume to 75 percent.
