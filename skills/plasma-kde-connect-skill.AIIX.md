# Plasma-KDE-Connect-Skill  
### _plasma-kde-connect-skill.AIIX_  
## About:  
Control your KDE-Connect connected devices with actions such as:
- Find My Phone
- Ping My Phone
- Browse Phone Files
- Dictate and Send an SMS to Your Google Contacts.
